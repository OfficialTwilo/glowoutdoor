5 significant benefits: Low CPM Taxi top advertising has the lowest cost per thousand impressions because of how cheap it is relative to the amount of people who see it. Taxis are always moving and attract a large audience due to the places they are mostly traveling to and from. For example, taxis are usually found in cities of high population, airports, and shopping complexes. At these locations, it is most common for people to demonstrate a purchasing behavior in response to the advertisements that they see.

Reach the Entire Market Taxi top advertisements are seen by consumers of every demographic. This ensures that all types of consumers of different geographic and socioeconomic locations have the same opportunity of viewing an advertisement. Taxis are also most commonly active in crowded areas or cities where shopping is prevalent. This is an effective way to lead consumers to buying a specific product.

Eye-Level View Not only do taxi top advertisements get the attention of the person getting into the cab, but also the consumers that the cab drives by. The height of the advertisements on taxi tops is exactly at eye-level, which forces consumers to constantly view them as the taxis drive by them. Consumers are mostly forced into seeing advertisements on taxis, as opposed to on TV where they can just ignore them. This makes them highly effective and dominant in crowded cities.

Good Consumer Recall According to OAAA, consumers recall products and advertisers best with taxi top advertising. Taxi top advertisements allow repetitive exposure to consumers, leading to higher consumer recognition and better purchase behavior. This is helpful in keeping a specific brand name in the mind of consumers, and forgetting about competitors.
